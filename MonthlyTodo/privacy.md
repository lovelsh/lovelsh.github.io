## English

### Privacy Policy

**Effective date**: 2026-05-24

**Service name**: MonthlyTodo (월간일정)

**Developer / Operator**: SeungHee. Lee

Monthly Todo (the "App") respects your privacy. This policy explains what data the App handles and how.

#### 1. Personal Information Collected
The App does not require account creation and **does not collect any personally identifiable information** such as name, email, phone number, or date of birth.

#### 2. What Is Stored and Where
- All data you enter — todos, group names, color/icon choices, font and layout settings — is stored **only on your device.**
- The App operates no server of its own and does not transmit user-entered data to any external server controlled by the developer.

#### 3. iCloud (CloudKit) Sync — Optional
- If you enable iCloud, data may be synchronized through Apple's CloudKit Private Database, accessible only by your own Apple ID.
- Such data is governed by Apple's iCloud terms. The developer of this App cannot access it.

#### 4. JSON Backup / Restore — Optional (Premium)
- This feature only runs when you explicitly trigger it. The App never creates an automatic backup in the background.
- **Where the backup file is created**: the file is written to the destination you pick in iOS's Files sheet (e.g., On My iPhone, iCloud Drive, Dropbox, Google Drive, or any storage you connected to the Files app). The App does not keep a copy on any developer-controlled server.
- **Operator access**: the resulting backup file resides only in your chosen storage. **The developer of this App cannot read, modify, or delete it.**
- **Restore flow**: when you pick a backup file, the App parses (JSON-decodes) it **entirely on-device**. The file's contents are never sent to an external server or to the developer.
- **Your responsibility for the file**: managing and deleting the backup file itself is your responsibility. Uninstalling the App does not automatically remove backup files saved to external storage — you must delete them yourself from the storage where they live.

#### 5. In-App Purchase
- The Premium feature (unlimited tasks + JSON backup & restore) is offered via Apple In-App Purchase (StoreKit).
- Payment is handled by Apple. The App does not collect or store payment information beyond the StoreKit receipt.

#### 6. Advertising / Analytics
The App **does not use any advertising SDKs or third-party analytics tools** and does not track user behavior.

#### 7. Permissions Used
- **Local Notifications**: used to remind you of your todos. Denying it disables only the reminder feature.

#### 8. Retention and Deletion
Because all data is stored on your device, removing the App deletes it. If you used iCloud sync, you can additionally delete it through iCloud settings. JSON backup files must be deleted manually by you, per §4.

#### 9. Your Rights
You can view, edit, and delete your data at any time within the App. You can export your data with the JSON backup feature (§4).

#### 10. Contact
- Responsible person: SeungHee. Lee
- Email: lovelsh.support@gmail.com

#### 11. Changes to This Policy
Any changes will be communicated through app updates and this page.
