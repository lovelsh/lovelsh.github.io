- 자녀 데이터는 모두 기기 내부에 저장 — 외부 서버로 전송되지 않습니다

## English

Support page for **Schedule Manager** (Korean app name: ScheMng / 자녀일정) — an iPhone app that turns preschool, daycare and school notices into per-child reminders.

- 🔒 [Privacy Policy (English)](./privacy_en.md)
- ❓ [Frequently Asked Questions (English)](./support_en.md)

### About the app

Schedule Manager captures the printed handouts and photos sent home from preschool, daycare or school, attaches them straight to a schedule entry, sorts them by category (School / Academy / Homework / Event / Supplies / Field Trip / Counseling / Other) and color-coded child profile, then fires a local reminder days in advance and surfaces the next item on a home-screen widget.

- iOS 17 or newer (SwiftUI / SwiftData / WidgetKit)
- Korean + English (1.0)
- All child, schedule and notice photo data stays on the device — nothing is sent to a server we operate
