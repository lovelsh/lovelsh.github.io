### About the App
SubCheck collects every subscription you pay for — streaming, music, shopping, cloud, AI tools, and telecom bundles — into a single dashboard. Get a push notification the day before each charge, see upcoming bills on a home-screen widget, and watch where your monthly spending really goes.
